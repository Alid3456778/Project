Eiffel Tower

About :

Iconic and enchanting, the Eiffel Tower stands as the symbol of Paris. A wrought-iron masterpiece, it offers panoramic views of the City of Lights. Whether admired from below or ascended for breathtaking vistas, this architectural gem captivates with its timeless elegance and romantic allure.

Booking

Images :

The Eiffel Tower, constructed in 1889 as the entrance arch to the 1889 World's Fair, has become an iconic symbol of Paris and one of the most recognized structures globally.

Designed by engineer Gustave Eiffel, the tower stands at a height of 324 meters (1,063 feet) and was the tallest man-made structure in the world at the time of its completion.

The Eiffel Tower's height can vary due to temperature changes. In hot weather, the iron expands, making the structure taller, while in cold weather, it contracts, causing a slight reduction in height..

Location:

The Eiffel Tower is located in the 7th arrondissement of Paris, France, on the Champ de Mars by the Seine River.

Attractions

1 .Observation Decks:

The Eiffel Tower has three levels, and the top two levels are accessible to the public. Visitors can take elevators or climb stairs to reach the observation decks, offering breathtaking views of Paris.

2 .Restaurants:

The tower is home to two restaurants, including the Michelin-starred "Le Jules Verne" on the second level.

3 .Night Illumination:

The Eiffel Tower is beautifully illuminated at night with sparkling lights, providing a spectacular view.

Best Time to Visit:

The Eiffel Tower is often used as a backdrop for various events and celebrations, including New Year's Eve fireworks and Bastille Day celebrations.
